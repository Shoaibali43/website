About Baseej Holidays

Baseej Travels is your trusted companion for Kashmir travel and holiday packages. With years of experience as a leading travel agency in Kashmir, we know how to turn vacations into unforgettable memories. From breathtaking valleys to snow-capped Himalayan peaks, our carefully designed Kashmir tour packages are created to give you experiences that truly stay with you.

We understand that every traveler is unique. That’s why we take the time to listen to what matters most to you—so your journey feels personal, not just planned. Whether it’s sipping a warm cup of Kahwa by Dal Lake, wandering through flower-filled Mughal Gardens, or finding peace in the quiet mountains of Gulmarg, Pahalgam, or Sonmarg, we make sure your Kashmir holiday feels just right for you.

With Baseej Travels, it’s not just about visiting Kashmir—it’s about living it. Explore at your own pace, in your own way, with a trusted Kashmir travel partner by your side.
